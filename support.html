Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a power outage?

You can report a power outage through our 24/7 helpline, mobile app, or by using our online portal. For immediate assistance, call our emergency number 1800-999-9999.

How can I dispute my bill?

To dispute your bill, you can file a complaint through our customer service portal or visit our nearest office. Make sure to have your consumer number and relevant bill details ready.

What are the payment options available?

We offer multiple payment options including online payment through our website/app, UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash payments at our authorized collection centers.
